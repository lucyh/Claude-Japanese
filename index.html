How to Practice

Choose a mode, then type your answers.

  • EN → JP: see English, type the romaji.
  • JP → EN: see Japanese, type the English.
  • Hiragana / Katakana: see the character, type the romaji.

Press Enter to check your answer, then Enter again (or tap Next) to continue.

At the end of a round, you can redo just the ones you missed.

To add your own words, edit the vocabulary array at the top of the <script> in index.html.
